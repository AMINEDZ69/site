About Us

Creation of the school

The National School of Advanced Technologies has just been created under Executive Decree No. 22-314 of September 12, 2022 published in Official Journal No. 78 . In the same issue of the Official Journal, Executive Decree No. 22-398 of November 21, 2022 is published, dissolving the National Higher School of Technology and the Higher School of Applied Sciences Algiers and transferring their assets, rights, obligations, personnel and resources to the National Higher School of Advanced Technologies. The School is a public establishment of a scientific, cultural and professional nature, with legal personality and financial autonomy. The school's mission is to provide higher education, scientific research and technological development in the various specialties of advanced technologies, particularly in industrial engineering, transport engineering, automation, electronics, telecommunications, mechanical engineering, electrical engineering and process engineering. From the entrance to the university for the year 2023-2024, In addition to the specialization of the school in the field of Sciences and Technologies , another field has been added, which is the field of Math Computer Science MI , very much in demand in the economic and practical fields. The school is a center of excellence for higher education that guarantees highly qualified training for the benefit of the various sectors of the country's economic and commercial activity.

School missions

As part of the public higher education service, the school provides higher education missions and missions of scientific research, innovation, monitoring, transfer and technological development.
